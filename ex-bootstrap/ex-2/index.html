Four Reasons why Japan is a Top Incentive Destination

New ideas to inspire business innovation.

World-renowned for its public safety, Japan's unique culture and customs will not fail to surprise and inspire its visitors.

Experience new standards of hospitality in Japan.

Omotenashi, the spirit of Japanese hospitality, is everywhere. Not only where it is expected - from accommodation facilities to transportation services - but also in every gesture, contributing to create a welcoming environment.

Soak in Japanese culture and learn new ways of enhancing teamwork.

From traditional festivals to collective cultural activites, from culinary experiences to group visits, Japan offers an abundance of tried and tested team building techniques.

In Japan, revering the bond that ties us to nature has been emphasized since antiquity.

Japan offers its visitors an endless variety of landscapes, climates and natural settings. All of which will morph to new forms following the four seasons so that there is always something new to discover.
